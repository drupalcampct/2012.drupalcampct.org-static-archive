Drupal Camp August 18th 2012

DrupalCampCT is a full-day, no-cost event, structured around the open-source content management system, Drupal, which includes knowledgeable speakers, informative workshops, impromptu birds-of-a-feather sessions, and invaluable opportunities to network with other Drupalists in and around the Nutmeg State!

We expect attendees to be a good mix of experienced Drupalists and newcomers to Drupal; developers, themers, content managers and project leaders; and consultants, corporate professionals, academics and passionate hobbists.

We are presently looking for sponsors for this year's DrupalCampCT event. Your support of DrupalCamps shows your respect for the value that community brings to Drupal as an open-source project. Learn how you can sponsor this event.

2012 Drupal Camp Keynote - John Eckman

Drupal Camp CT is happy to announce that this years keynote will be presented by John Eckman.

Delivering Results: The State of Content Management and the Opportunity for Drupal.

More Information about John

John Eckman is a veteran of the web content management industry, a blogger for the CMS Myth (www.cmsmyth.com), and the Managing Director of the Boston office of ISITE Design (www.isitedesign.com), He's worked with many open source and proprietary content management platforms large and small, and has been at various points a user experience lead, a project manager, a developer and technical architect, and a digital strategist for clients from 10 person non-profits to the Fortune 50. He's been a Drupal user since 2006 and has spoken at DrupalCamp Montreal and DrupalCamp Western MA, in addition to conferences like Web Content, Gilbane, New England GiveCamp, and CMS Expo. He's also the founding organizer of WordCamp Boston, but try not to hold that against him. He tweets as @jeckman, is on Github (github.com/jeckman) and D.O. (drupal.org/user/209083) as jeckman.
